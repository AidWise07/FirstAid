About AidWise

The first helping hand at your service!

AidWise brings to you the step by step instructions for your injuries! It tries to make sure you are fit as a fiddle to be your best. We try to include every possible need you might have at your finger tips!! It includes features like first aid administrating tips, buying first aid products, trying to contact nearby doctors and rides in the case of emergencies.

About AidWise

Let us give you an AidWise!

AidWise mainly tells you about injuries and helps you in administering first aid to injuries in the best way possible. It tries to provide you with accurate instructions through verified and trusted sources. We try to bring the best advice as a first aid!!!

About

AidWise is a final year project by the students of Department of Computer Science and Applications of St. Mary's College(Autonomous), Thrissur. This project tries to provide features and resources so that people can handle medical emergencies in a better way.
